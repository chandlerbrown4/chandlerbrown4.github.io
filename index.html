About Me

Hello! I'm Chandler Brown, a recent graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Appalachian State University. I'm currently on an exciting journey to become a penetration tester, actively working towards my OSCP certification, which I expect to complete in August 2024.

My passion for technology and cybersecurity is matched by a diverse skill set that includes proficiency in tools like Git, VS Code, Nessus, Nmap, Burp Suite, and AWS. I am well-versed in multiple programming languages, including Java, Python, Haskell, R, C, C++, HTML, CSS, and PowerShell. My experience spans from hands-on IT internships, where I managed secure data destruction and automated IT processes, to managerial roles in the service industry, where I honed my leadership and communication skills.

Outside of my professional pursuits, I have a love for coding, cooking, and hiking. Whether I'm exploring new trails or experimenting with a new recipe, I bring the same curiosity and dedication to my hobbies as I do to my work in cybersecurity.

I'm excited to connect with like-minded professionals and explore opportunities to make a significant impact in the field of cybersecurity. Feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn or via email at evan.brown.us@gmail.com. Let's build something great together!
